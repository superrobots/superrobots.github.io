Welcome!

We are the SuperRobots team, passionate about building intelligent and helpful robots. Our mission is to create technology that improves lives and inspires the next generation of innovators.

SuperRobots is a First Lego League (FLL) Challenge competitive team, Team ID #70620.

Our team is made up of 5th graders from Soleado Elementary School, except Jeremy, who is from Silverspur Elementary School. Rigel is our youngest member and is in 3rd grade. We share a passion for robotics and learning, and each of us brings unique talents and interests to the team!

2023 SuperPowered Season

FIRST LEGO League SuperPowered challenged teams to explore how energy is generated, stored, distributed, and consumed. Teams designed, built, and programmed LEGO robots to complete missions on a themed field, focusing on innovative solutions for energy challenges in our world.

  • Learned about renewable and non-renewable energy sources.
  • Designed and built a robot to complete energy-themed missions.
  • Developed a project to improve energy efficiency in our community.
  • Practiced teamwork, problem-solving, and presentation skills.

Learn more about SuperPowered

2024 Masterpiece Season

FIRST LEGO League Masterpiece invites teams to imagine and innovate in the world of creative arts and entertainment. Teams use STEM and robotics to solve challenges related to sharing their passions and talents with the world, making performances and art more accessible and engaging.

  • Explored how technology can enhance creative expression and communication.
  • Built and programmed robots to complete missions inspired by the arts.
  • Created a project to help people share their stories and talents.
  • Strengthened collaboration, creativity, and technical skills.

Learn more about Masterpiece

2025 Unearthed Season

FIRST LEGO League Unearthed is the 2025-2026 season challenge, where teams will dig into the fascinating world beneath our feet! Teams will explore how we interact with the ground, discover underground mysteries, and use STEM and robotics to solve real-world problems related to geology, archaeology, and the environment.

  • Investigate underground resources and how they impact our lives.
  • Design and program robots to complete missions inspired by digging, discovery, and preservation.
  • Develop innovative solutions for sustainable use of underground spaces.
  • Enhance teamwork, research, and creative problem-solving skills.

Learn more about Unearthed
